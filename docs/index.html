Steam Analyzer

This website will analyze your Steam games by playtime. You must enter your 64-bit steamid in the input below. (You can get your 64-bit steamid by visiting steamid.io). Click example to see a sample display using the web designer's steamID.

Your Steam profile must also be publicly visible -- on the "your profile" section in Steam, click "edit profile", and then click "my privacy settings" This website will NOT store any data including steamids or games owned.
